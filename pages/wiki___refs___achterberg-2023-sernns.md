- **Title:** Spatially Embedded Recurrent Neural Networks Reveal Widespread Links between Structural and Functional Neuroscience Findings
- **Authors:** Achterberg, J., et al.
- **Year:** 2023
- **Key idea:** Loss = L_task + λ Σ |w_ij| d_ij. Physical distance penalty on weights leads to spontaneous emergence of modular, small-world topologies similar to biological connectomes.
- **Relevance:** Direct predecessor to [[wiki/research/spatial-neuromorphic-priors]]. Reported structure-function alignment (later contrasted with our null result on 2D-PVR). Motivates the "brain economy" approach to training.
