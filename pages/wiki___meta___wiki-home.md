- # Hello there 🌻
	- You have reached my personal wiki vault!
	- This is a living knowledge base on **neural cellular automata**, **self-organising boolean circuits**, **modularity**, and **brain networks** — the research threads I work on as a postdoc at the [Institute of Neuroinformatics (INI), UZH/ETH Zurich](https://www.ini.uzh.ch/).
	- It is very much a **work in progress**, aimed at growing and improving organically. Pages will appear and deepen over time as I read, write, and think. Stay tuned! 🌱
- ## What is this?
	- A compiled knowledge base rather than a chat log. The idea (from Andrej Karpathy): instead of asking an LLM the same question repeatedly, distil the answer once into an interconnected page and keep it current.
	- Pages are distilled from papers, code, and research notes using [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/code). Every page carries a **confidence score** (how well-sourced it is) and a **lifecycle state** — only pages I have personally read and approved reach this public wiki.
	- Provenance markers flag inference: `^[inferred]` means a claim goes beyond the source; `^[ambiguous]` means sources disagree.
